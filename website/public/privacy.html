Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how My Habit Streak collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

My Habit Streak is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal data, usage statistics, or any other information from our users. All your habit data is stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use your information in any way.

Data Security

Your habit data is stored securely on your device. We do not transmit it to any external servers. This means your data is always under your control.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
